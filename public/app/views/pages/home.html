Welcome to Table Tome

Table Tome is the entire Dungeons & Dragons toolbox at your fingertips. We aim to streamline gameplay by providing quick spell and class look up, easy character sheet development, and more.

Open the Tome

Get new players acquainted with the game faster

As a new player, it takes time to understand all of the different roles of Dungeons and Dragons. It takes countless hours of thumbing through spellbooks to understand your role. By providing an easy way to quickly look up components of the game, we hope to get new people playing and having fun at a faster pace.

Custom Spellbook Design

Veterans of the game love to personalize their game. We allow for custom created spellbooks, classes and more.

Find new ways to play your game.

Look up custom game components, such as spellbooks, player classes, and storylines, created by other users. Implement them into your own game, or post your own works to be used by the community!
