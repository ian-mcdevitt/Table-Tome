Raising Accessibility

Dungeons & Dragons features a complex rule set, developed over the course of 40 years. It is multifaceted, and takes dedication to master. Tome brings pieces of the Dungeons & Dragons rule set into a comfortable and easy to navigate format.

Faster than Flipping

The Official Player's Handbook features 300+ content dense pages. Spells alone spread accross 91; The growing list of official companion rule sets only increases this page count. By reformatting and reorganizing this wealth of arcane knowledge, players can spend less time with their noses' in the book, and more time with their heads' in the game.

All Content Included

Pending contact with Wizards of the Coast, should a user possess an Official Player's Handbook, Tome will provide access to the entire collection of Dungeons & Dragons spells. Due to copyright limitations, Tome cannot reproduce content outside of the publicly released rule sets.

Sharing is Caring

While loose leaf paper can get the job done, Tome keeps all of your custom content in one, univerally accessible location. Tome will allow an inspired player to format and organize their ideas for both personal and community use.
