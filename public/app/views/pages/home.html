Welcome to Table Tome

Table Tome is an entire Dungeons & Dragons toolbox at your fingertips. We aim to streamline gameplay by providing quick spell and class look up, easy character sheet development, and more.

Open the Tome

Goals of the Tome

Get new players acquainted with the game faster.

As a new player, it takes time to understand all of the different roles of Dungeons and Dragons. It takes countless hours of thumbing through spellbooks to get a grasp of the gameplay. By providing an easy way to quickly look up components of the game, we hope to get new people playing and having fun at a faster pace.

Find new ways to play your game.

Look up custom game components, such as spellbooks, player classes, and storylines, created by other users. Implement them into your own game, or post your own works to be used by the community!

Streamline gameplay.

By lessening the time it takes to do things such as looking up spells and updating character info, you spend more time doing what you love: playing. We hope to provide users a faster paced Dungeons & Dragons experience, to keep you on the edge of your seat in excitement.

What it has

A preliminary spellbook

We have currently implemented the basic spellbook, provided for free by Wizards of the Coast. This can be accessed by anyone, by simply clicking the 'spellbook' tab at the top of the screen.

Easy search and lookup

Our database allows lookup based on class, level, and school. Easily combine different paramaters to find the spells that you are looking for.

Where it's going

Full 5th edition support

Pending contact with Wizards of the Coast, we are working on implementing a system to access the entire paid spellbook online. If we can work with Wizards of the Coast to verify ownership of the physical spellbook, we can allow you to access all spells, classes, and content from the 5th edition spellbook here, at the touch of your fingertips.

Custom Spellbook Design

Veterans of the game love to personalize and taylor the game to themselves. We want to allow for the creation and sharing of these personalized spellbooks, classes and more.

User profiles and content

Create an account to share your own content, find new content to use yourself, and more. We want to build a friendly community for the purpose of sharing gameplay ideas, in order to enhance the gameplay experiences of everyone.
